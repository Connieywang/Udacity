What is the structure of your dataset?¶

There are 94802 individual rides in the dataset with 16 features(duration_sec,start_station_id, user_type, etc) at the beginning. After cleaning null data for member_birth_year, it has 86963 rows now. Most variables are numeric in nature, but variables like bike_share_for_all_trip, user_type,and end_station_name are categorcial.

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?¶

I am interested in figuring out what features made the large proportion of bike usage for Ford Gobike in January.

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?¶

I expect that user_type will have the strongest effect on bike usage: If people are Subscriber, they are more likely would use GoBike frequently. Also, I believe hour, age, and location have effects on the bike usage.
